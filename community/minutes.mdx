# Meeting minutes

This is the historical archive of all the conda-forge core meeeting minutes up to December 2025.
In January 2026, the core meetings were [merged](/news/2025/12/22/new-meetings-schedule/) with the wider conda community meetings.
As a result, the minutes are kept in the [meeting notes archive of conda.org](https://conda.org/community/meetings).
