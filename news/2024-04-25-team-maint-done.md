# Feedstock Team Update Maintenance Completed

We have completed maintenance on our feedstock GitHub team infrastructure. You may
notice a new `.recipe_maintainers.json` file in feedstocks you maintain. This file is
autogenerated and managed by our infrastructure. If you edit it manually or remove
it, you may loose access to the feedstock. If you need help with a feedstock
team-related maintenance item, please contact `@conda-forge/core`.
